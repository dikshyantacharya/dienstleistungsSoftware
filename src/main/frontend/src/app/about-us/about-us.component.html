This is a Dummy-Website created by me with an intention of testing my combinative and practical knowlege of Angular, HTML5, CSS3, TypeScript , Java, Hibernate, SpringBoot and Intellij.

What is the intention of this site?

Dienstleistung is a dummy website that allows users to post their jobs and to request the job .

How does this site work?

  • First you have to create an account by clicking Create New Account where every feild in the form has to be filled.The password must be atleast 8 characters long and email should be valid. The email adderss should be unique.
  • Enter your email and password and click Log In. You will be redirected into home.
  • Now you can add the service by clicking "Dienst Erstellen" where u have to fill the input feild of the form that will be displayed.The price should contain only numbers and it will be displayed as Euro.
  • After adding service in your account, u can now click "Meine Dienst" where u can see all the services that you have added. Now you can update, delete or share with the other users if u want.
  • Based on share and unshare you can see the services that are shared by you.
  • You can click the notification inorder to see the request from other user who is interested in the service you have shared .
  • Likewise, you can also see the books shared by other users in home. Just click the service you want to have and you will be redirected to the page where the information about the service and the user will be showed. If you are interested at that service you can click the Accept button and the user will be notified. If you are not interested at that service you can click the Unaccept button. Further more, you can see the email of that person and if you click , email app will be opened with already filled email subject and salutation. Further more you can delete your account and upate your information change your password and logout.
